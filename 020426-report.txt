

Work Summary — done April 2. What was done

Developed and launched a new company website at bostonelitecontractors.com as a separate domain, preserving the existing Wix presence at elitecontractorsllc.biz without modifications.
Website Development
Designed and built a complete multi-page site covering Services, Gallery, About, and Contact. Services were structured into seven categorized sections aligned with how customers search online: Home Remodeling & Renovations, Roofing & Siding, Interior Repairs & Finishes, Landscaping & Tree Services, Solar Energy Solutions, Pest Control, and Consulting. An interactive photo gallery with lightbox viewer was implemented to showcase completed projects. Contact information, social media links, and lead-generation calls-to-action were integrated throughout.
SEO & Digital Marketing Setup
Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager were configured for visitor tracking and campaign measurement. Google Search Console was connected to enable search indexing and monitor organic search performance. Each service category was mapped to targeted keywords to maximize visibility in local search results.
Performance Optimization
Site images were compressed across all service categories to reduce page load times and improve user experience.
