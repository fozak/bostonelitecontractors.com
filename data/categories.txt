1. Use Primary Service Categories

Group services under broad headings. These become your main navigation or headings on your website, and each can target a primary keyword.

Example Classification:

A. Home Remodeling & Renovations
Keywords for SEO: kitchen remodeling, bathroom renovation, room additions, basement finishing, exterior renovations

Kitchen Remodeling: custom cabinetry, countertops, flooring, appliance installations
Bathroom Remodeling: vanities, tile work, fixtures, full renovations
Room Additions: living space expansion, new room construction
Basement Finishing: turning basements into livable spaces
Exterior Renovations: siding, roofing, porches, patios

B. Roofing & Siding
Keywords: roof repair, roof replacement, siding repair, shingle replacement

Roof Repair & Replacement: leak repair, shingle replacement, maintenance
Siding Repair & Installation: damaged siding replacement

C. Interior Repairs & Finishes
Keywords: drywall repair, painting services, flooring repair, window repair

Drywall & Painting: wall patching, full interior painting
Flooring Repair: hardwood, tile, carpet repair or replacement
Window & Door Repair: installation, replacement, energy-efficient upgrades

D. Landscaping & Tree Services
Keywords: tree removal, tree cutting, stump grinding, landscaping services

Tree Removal & Stump Grinding
Landscaping: trimming, planting, outdoor aesthetics

E. Solar Energy Solutions
Keywords: solar panel installation, residential solar systems, solar energy design

Custom Solar System Design
Solar Panel Installation & Connection

F. Pest Control Services
Keywords: eco-friendly pest control, home pest services, commercial pest management

Residential & Commercial Pest Control
Safe, eco-friendly treatments

G. Consulting & Project Management
Keywords: construction consulting, project management services, building permits

Free Initial Consultations
Project Planning & Management
Permitting & Compliance Assistance
